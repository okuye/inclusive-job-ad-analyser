# Example: Inclusive Job Ad

This is an example of a job description using inclusive, neutral language.

---

## Senior Software Engineer

We're seeking a **skilled professional** to join our **collaborative team** at a growing technology company.

### About the Role:
You'll work on challenging technical problems in a **dynamic, evolving environment** where innovation and **diverse perspectives** are valued.

### Requirements:
- **2+ years of experience or equivalent skills** in software development
- **Proactive problem solver** with strong analytical abilities
- **Effective communicator** who works well in teams
- **Comfortable with technology** and eager to learn new tools
- Experience with modern development practices

### What We're Looking For:
- Strong technical foundation in software engineering
- **Driven** and **results-focused** mindset
- Ability to work both independently and collaboratively
- Enthusiasm for tackling complex challenges

### What We Offer:
- **Competitive salary and benefits**
- **Flexible working arrangements** (remote/hybrid options)
- Comprehensive **parental leave** policy
- Professional development opportunities
- **Accommodations available** for all team members
- Inclusive, supportive work environment

### Our Commitment:
We are an **equal opportunity employer** committed to building a **diverse** and **inclusive** workplace. We welcome applicants from **all backgrounds** and provide a supportive environment where everyone can thrive.

Candidates with **equivalent experience** through alternative paths (bootcamps, self-taught, career changers) are strongly encouraged to apply.

---

## Positive Aspects:

### Inclusive Language:
✓ "skilled professional" instead of "rockstar"
✓ "collaborative team" instead of "young and energetic"  
✓ "2+ years or equivalent skills" allows for diverse backgrounds
✓ "proactive" instead of "aggressive"
✓ "comfortable with technology" instead of "digital native"
✓ "dynamic, evolving" instead of "fast-paced"

### Positive Indicators:
✓ Equal opportunity employer statement
✓ Mentions diversity and inclusion explicitly
✓ Flexible working arrangements
✓ Accommodations available
✓ Parental leave
✓ All backgrounds welcome
✓ Equivalent experience accepted
✓ Competitive salary (proper context)

### Neutral Language:
✓ No gendered terms
✓ No age restrictions or implications
✓ No ableist language
✓ No culture-fit coded language
✓ No socioeconomic requirements

**Expected Score:** Excellent (90-100/100)
